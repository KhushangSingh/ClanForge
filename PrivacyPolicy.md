At ClanForge, accessible from [Insert Your Website URL], one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by ClanForge and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide
When you register for an account, we may ask for your:

Personal Information: Name, Email Address.

Profile Details: Bio, Portfolio URL, LinkedIn URL, GitHub URL, and Phone Number (Optional).

Authentication Data: Passwords (hashed and secured) or Google OAuth tokens.

B. Information Collected via Google OAuth
If you choose to sign in with Google:

We collect your Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture hosted by Google.

We do not access your contacts, Google Drive files, or other private data.

C. Usage Data
We collect data on how you interact with the app, such as:

Squads (Lobbies) you create or join.

Date and time of your requests.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:

Provide, operate, and maintain our website.

Authentication: Verify your identity via OTP (Email) or Google Sign-In.

Collaboration: Allow other users to see your profile when you join a squad so they can connect with you.

Communication: Send you verification codes (OTPs) and critical account updates.

3. How We Share Your Information
Public Profile: Your Name, Avatar, Bio, and Social Links are visible to other logged-in users when you join a squad.

Host Contact Info: If you are a Squad Host and explicitly toggle "Show Contact Info," your Email and Phone Number will be visible to members of that specific squad.

No Third-Party Sales: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Data Security
We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Passwords are encrypted.

Verification is handled via secure OTPs.

Google Sign-In tokens are verified directly with Google servers.

5. Your Data Rights
You have the right to:

Access: View your profile data at any time.

Edit: Update your profile details via the "Edit Profile" feature.

Delete: You can delete your account permanently via the Settings menu. This will remove your personal data and disband squads you host.

6. Cookies and Local Storage
ClanForge uses Local Storage to maintain your login session (clanforge_user). We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.
